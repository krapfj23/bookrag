## The Ask

As a next step in the process, we want you to record a video for us walking us through a live demo of a project you’ve built. The project should be something you are proud of building and have done 90%+ of the work on. Guidelines for the video:

- Only 1-3 minutes in length.
- Shows off your project and tells us why you’re proud of it. Why is this interesting? Why does it show you’re a great software engineer?
- Walks us through the live project, and explains how you built it.
- Shows off your excitement for the project, to give us a preview of what it will be like to work together.
- (optional) start or end the video by telling a joke.

We’d like you to submit your video by Tuesday, April 28th, and we’ll get back to you with our decision on next steps by Thursday, April 30th. 

Submit your video as a youtube unlisted video and email the link to [northeastern@subconscious.dev](mailto:northeastern@subconscious.dev) with a subject “CO-OP CHALLENGE”
